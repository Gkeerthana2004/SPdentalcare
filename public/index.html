What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

State-of-the-art treatments tailored to your unique smile, delivered with precision and care.

Book a Service →
🩺

General Check-up

Comprehensive oral health examinations including digital X-rays, professional cleaning, and personalised care plans for lasting health.

🪥

Scaling

Professional removal of plaque and tartar deposits to prevent tooth decay, gum disease, and maintain a bright, healthy smile.

⚙️

Dental Filling

Tooth-colored resin or composite fillings that restore decayed teeth and maintain natural appearance with superior durability.

🔬

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacements that look, feel, and function exactly like your natural teeth, with titanium roots built to last a lifetime.

🛡️

Root Canal Treatment

Pain-free root canal treatments using rotary endodontics and 3D imaging for precise, comfortable outcomes and long-lasting results.

😁

Orthodontics

Modern braces and clear aligner systems to straighten your teeth discreetly and comfortably, no matter your age.

🧹

Scaling & Root Planing (SRP)

Deep cleaning treatment for gum disease that removes tartar and plaque buildup both above and below the gum line for optimal oral health.

👑

Zirconia Crowns

Premium all-ceramic crowns offering superior aesthetics and durability, perfect for visible teeth with exceptional biocompatibility.

🏆

Metal Ceramic Crown

Durable crowns combining metal strength with ceramic aesthetics, ideal for back teeth requiring both strength and appearance.

🦷

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-colored braces that are virtually invisible yet highly effective for straightening teeth with discreet orthodontic treatment.

⚡

Metal Braces

Traditional, highly effective braces using advanced technology for quick and reliable teeth straightening at affordable prices.

📍

Clear Aligners

Custom-made transparent aligners for discreet teeth straightening without wires or brackets, removable for easy cleaning.

👶

Child Dental Care

Specialized pediatric dentistry with kid-friendly approach, including preventive treatments, fillings, and oral health education.

🔧

FLAP Surgery

Advanced periodontal surgical procedure for treating severe gum disease and bone loss with tissue regeneration techniques.

⚕️

Orthognathic Surgery

Corrective jaw surgery to address severe bite problems and jaw misalignment, improving function and facial aesthetics.

🦷

Impaction - Wisdom Tooth Removal

Expert surgical extraction of impacted wisdom teeth with minimal discomfort, using advanced techniques for faster recovery.

🌉

Fixed Partial Denture (FPD) - Bridging

Custom-crafted dental bridges that restore your smile by replacing missing teeth with natural-looking, permanent fixed prosthetics.

😁

Complete Denture

Full arch dentures designed for perfect fit and natural aesthetics, restoring complete oral function and your confident smile.

💎

Fixed Implant Supported Complete Denture

Permanently fixed full-arch restoration supported by dental implants for unmatched stability, function, and natural appearance.

🔧

Removable Implant Supported Overdenture

Snap-on overdenture retained by implants offering superior retention and stability while remaining removable for easy cleaning.
